"Threshold progress" is a statutory measure, not a score.

It is simply custody days served ÷ the BNSS s.479 threshold that applies to that person (⅓ of the maximum sentence for first-time offenders, ½ otherwise). It involves no weighting, prediction, or judgment — see Methodology.
